This mod adds additional levels of ascension beyond the vanilla ones. 
It's strongly recommended that you play on vanilla asc 15 in addition to this mod's ascension level. 
Mod ascension level can be changed in the mod config.

Ascension+ 1: Shop removal service now starts at a cost of 100 instead of 75.
Ascension+ 2: Ascender's bane is no longer ethereal.
Ascension+ 3: You gain 20% less gold.
Ascension+ 4: Curses are no longer removable.
Ascension+ 5: Healing is 25% less effective
