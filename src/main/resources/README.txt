This mod adds additional levels of ascension beyond the vanilla ones. You unlock the levels through normal ascension progression.

Ascension+ 1 (21): Shop removal service now starts at a cost of 100 instead of 75.
Ascension+ 2 (22): You gain 15% less gold.
Ascension+ 3 (23): Healing is 20% less effective.
Ascension+ 4 (24): Ascender's bane is no longer ethereal.
Ascension+ 5 (25): Opening boss chests now gives you a random curse.
